1 · Phase Timeline

Each square marks one training session, coloured by phase (P0 = acclimation with drifting target, P1 = fixed walls ±8 with block-level sign switches, P2 = probabilistic side switching). Where two files share the same date the session with the most completed trials is kept.

2 · Session Duration

Total session duration in minutes, taken from the last completed-trial timestamp. Coloured by training phase.

3 · Trials per Session

Number of completed trial slots per session — trials where the mouse received a final outcome (hit or miss). Does not count repeated corridor runs within a single slot.

3b · Total Attempts

Total corridor traversals per session, including every repeated run before a final outcome. Computed as Σ (1 + trials_repeated) across all completed trial slots. A slot where the mouse failed twice before hitting counts as 3 traversals.

4 · Hit Rate — Overall

Session hit rate = hits ÷ total attempts (counting repeats). Grey bars show N completed trial slots (right axis). Dashed line at 0.5 = chance. Coloured by training phase.

5 · Hit Rate — L vs R

Hit rate split by target side (door_x < 0 = left, door_x > 0 = right), computed per attempt. Background shading encodes training phase. door_x is recorded for all phases in this cohort.

6 · Side Bias Index

Bias index = right hit rate − left hit rate. Positive = better performance on the right side. Bar border colour encodes training phase.
